Tap is a adventure game where the player must press the spacebar to jump over obstacles and avoid falling into pits. The game features simple graphics and addictive gameplay, making it a fun and challenging experience for players of all ages.

#### Why named "Tap"?

Tap is not just a game — it's an experience from my learning journey in game development. The name "Tap" reflects the core mechanic of the game: tapping the spacebar to jump. It's a simple yet engaging action that encapsulates the essence of the gameplay.

- **Completely Free**: Enjoy all the features without spending a dime.
- **Family-Friendly**: Suitable for all age groups, making it perfect for everyone.
- **Visually Stunning**: Designed using high-quality free assets from the Unity Store to deliver a beautiful gaming experience.

Tap is lightweight and runs seamlessly on any operating system that supports the .NET runtime.
